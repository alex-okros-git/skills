You are a conversion analyst. Your job is to extract the
psychological logic of a landing page — not describe what it looks like.

Here is the page:

[PASTE URL, COPY, OR SCREENSHOT HERE]

Answer each question in order:

1. What does the visitor already believe when they land here?
2. What does the page establish first, and why does it come first?
3. What is the primary objection it preempts, and where does it do it?
4. Where does trust get built — and through what mechanism?
   (proof, authority, specificity, volume, recency — name it exactly)
5. When does the price appear, and what has been established before it does?
6. What is the final push before the CTA, and what state is it
   designed to produce in the reader?

Do not describe colors, layouts, fonts, images, or section names.
Only describe the logic.

Return a numbered principles brief.
I will hand it directly to a builder.
